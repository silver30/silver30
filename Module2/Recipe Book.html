Coconut Rice Recipe

Coconut is high in dietary fiber, helps to improve heart health, helps to prevent obesity, reduces sugar craving,
acts as energy booster and improves digestion.
this makes coconut rice a healthy choice

Coconut Rice recipe
Ingredients Quantity
coconut milk 2 cans
Rice 4 cups
green pepper 1
Red pepper 1
Shrimps 250g
Vegetable and coconut oil 5tbsp each
big carrots 3
Salt to taste

Cooking Procedures

  1. Wash the rice untill the water is clear
  2. Cut all veggies and set aside
  3. Wash chicken, season and cook untill tender
  4. heat the oil, add chopped onions to saute
  5. Add salt,ginger, garlic and seasoning cubes
  6. Add chicken stock and coconut milk, boil for 5minutes
  7. Add the washed rice, cover with foil paper and cook over medium heat and set aside
  8. Heat vegetable and coconut oil in a pan, add the vegies and fry on low heat, set aside
  9. In the sam deep pan, add the vegies and rice bit by bit and mix well
  10. Cover and allow to simmer.
  11. Serve with any choiced drink

SPRING GREEN FRIED RICE AND EGGS

Greens are important in a healthy diet, this is because they provide vitamin C, to support the immune system, and vitamin K, to build bone strength
Adding spring greens to rice makes it healthier
Here is a recepice for this yummy meal

Spring green rice and eggs recipe

Ingredients Quantity
Cooked rice 250g
Eggs 2
Spring onions 2
Spring greens 2 stalks
Soy sauce 1 tbsp
Sesame seed 1 tbsp
chopped coriander 1 bunch

Cooking Procedures

  1. Heat the oil in a pan. Swirl the eggs to cover the base of the pan to make a sort of egg pancake,
    when cooked through slide onto a plate and set aside
  2. Cook the spring onions and greens for a few mins in the same pan
  3. Add the rice and cook for a few minutes to heat through the egg pancake and mix through the rice
  4. Mix together the soy, sesame oil, coriander, sesame seeds, lime juice and sliced chilli for the dressing
  5. Toss the dressing through the rice to serve.

Beetroot Rice Recipe

Beets are loaded with vitamins and minerals yet low in calories and fat. They’re also a good source of several key nutrients, including folate, manganese, and copper.
Adding beets to diet aids digestion,improve heart and brain health. Now that you know this fact, lets add beets to your rice for that colourful meal.

Beetroot Rice Recipe

Ingredients Quantity
Basmatic rice 1 cup
Chopped beetroots 1cup
Greated beetroot 2tbsp
Sliced onions 1/2 bulb
Salt 1 tbsp
Ginger and garlic paste 1 tbsp
bay leaf 1

Cooking Procedures

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of vegetable oil add cumin,leaf, cloves, and cinnamon.
  2. Add ½ onion, chilli and ginger garlic paste. saute well.
  3. Add chopped beetroot and grated beetroot. saute for 2minutes
  4. Add salt, saute until the spices turn aromatic
  5. Now add basmati rice, 2 cup water and mix well
  6. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or till the rice get cooked completely
  7. Add 2 tbsp coriander and mix well
  8. Finally, beetroot rice is ready to serve with any protein of your choice.
